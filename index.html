Hello! Welcome to the BAPS heathy eating club website.

The benifits of having a healthy diet:

  • More energy
  • Potential to live longer
  • Better muscle gain
  • Better immune system

What a healthy diet might look like:

  • Vegetables, meat, and friuts evenly portioned on your plate
  • Not to many calories in a day
  • Not to much salt, not to much fat on your plate
  • Making sure you take care of your fibre intake
